FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Techopedia Lvl 12?

The twelfth edition of IEEE SIES GST’s annual technical fest.

What does the theme “PIXELS” imply?

The theme revolves around the exciting world of gaming.

Benefits of participating?

Cash prizes, coupons, goodies, national-level certificates, the title “Techopedia Level 12 Winner,” and more.

Registration fee?

₹80 for teams of 2 (Inquisitive, Squabble, Eureka). ₹180 for teams of 3 (Warlash).

Is on-day registration open?

Yes, on-day registration is available for Warlash. Ensure you register before the closing date for everything else.
